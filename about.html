About Us

What is GuideXP?

Through our digital platform, visitors can now unpack the stories behind the art pieces and learn more about the artist’s creative inspiration and process. We understand the importance of artists connecting with their audiences, and it is our mission to make sharing stories as simple as possible. With GuideXP, artists can easily create audio guides for their own exhibition, and engage their audience in more ways.
